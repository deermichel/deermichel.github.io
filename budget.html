About

Budget is a stripped-down tool to keep your daily expenses under control. Define your own categories and optionally set a budget for them. Over the month, record items in each category and revisit the app to gain a quick overview over your financial situation.

The key features include:
- Monthly budget overview
- Define custom categories
- Straightforward & clean design
- More coming soon...

If you're missing a feature or found a bug, don't hesitate to reach out and we can make things happen ;).

Privacy Policy

We do not collect, use, save or have access to any of your personal data recorded in Budget.
