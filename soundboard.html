About

soundboard is a live-performance music playground inviting everybody to get creative and to experiment with sounds. It is designed to be straightforward to use and to be understandable without knowing all the muso slang.

The key features include:
- On-screen keyboard with tone controls
- Support for external MIDI controllers
- More coming soon...

If you're missing a feature or found a bug, don't hesitate to reach out and we can make things happen ;).

Privacy Policy

soundboard does not collect, transmit, distribute or sell your data.
